Reproducing Kernel Hilbert Space

Let us begin with the definition of Hilbert spaces.

Definition

A Hilbert Space is an inner product space that is complete and separable with respect to the norm defined by the inner product.

A reproducing kernel Hilbert space (RKHS) is a family of functions on some set \(\mathcal{X}\) that forms a Hilbert space, with an associated kernel.

To start with, let us definne what we mean by a kernel. We will stick to Euclidean feature spaces \(\mathcal{X}\), although everything works out if \(\mathcal{X}\) is an arbitrary separable metric space.
